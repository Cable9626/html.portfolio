2011 Mercedes‑Benz C63 AMG BLACK SERIES W204 (2011-2013)

The Mercedes‑Benz C63 AMG Black Series (W204) is one of the most extreme and highly sought‑after performance cars AMG ever made — a race‑bred C‑Class that’s also road‑legal and rare.

What It Is

The Black Series is the ultimate high‑performance version of the W204‑generation C63 AMG, produced between 2011 and 2013. It was built by Mercedes‑AMG’s Performance Studio as a low‑volume, track‑focused model based on the C63 AMG Coupé.

Engine & Perfomance

  • Engine: 6.2‑litre naturally aspirated V8 (M156), hand‑built and tuned for maximum performance.
  • Power: ~380 kW (≈ 517 PS / ≈ 510 hp) and 620 Nm torque.
  • Transmission: 7‑speed AMG Speedshift MCT automatic, sending power to the rear wheels.
  • Acceleration: ~0–100 km/h in around 4.2 seconds.
  • Top Speed: Electronically limited to about 300 km/h (186 mph).

Chassis & Handling

  • Aggressive bodywork with flared wheel arches, larger air intakes, and a DTM‑inspired stance.
  • Aerodynamic enhancements, including optional carbon fibre splitters and wings.
  • Inside you’ll find lightweight sport seats (often 2‑seat layout), a sport steering wheel, red accents and Alcantara trim — less luxury‑oriented and more performance‑centric than regular C63 interiors.
