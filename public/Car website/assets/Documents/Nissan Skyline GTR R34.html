NISSAN SKYLINE GTR R34

Basic Overview

  • Produced: 1999–2002
  • Body styles: 2-door coupe & 4-door sedan (GT-R was coupe only)

Engine & Performance

  • Engine: 2.6L twin-turbo inline-6 (RB26DETT)
  • Official power: 276 hp (because of Japan’s “gentlemen’s agreement”)
  • Closer to 320+ hp stock
  • 0–60 mph: ~4.9 seconds
  • Top speed: Electronically limited to 155 mph (easily removable)

The RB26 is famous for being insanely overbuilt, which is why people push it to 600, 800, even 1,000+ hp with the right mods.

Tech That Was Way Ahead of Its Time

  • ATTESA E-TS AWD system – rear-wheel drive most of the time, sends power to the front when needed
  • Super-HICAS – rear-wheel steering for better cornering
  • Multi-Function Display (MFD) – showed boost, G-forces, lap times, oil temp, etc. (very futuristic in the late ’90s)
